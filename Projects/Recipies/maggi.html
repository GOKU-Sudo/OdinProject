Maggi

Ingredients

  • Maggi
  • Water
  • Maggi masala
  • Veggies

Steps

  1. Take a pot and boil 300ml water
  2. Now add your maggi noodles in it and let them boil for 3-4 minutes
  3. Take a fry pan and add a bit of oil and then fry the veggies little bit
  4. Now add your maggi masala in it and stir it
  5. Now add your noodles and mix it well then turn off the heat after a minute
  6. Your maggi is ready serve it hot.
