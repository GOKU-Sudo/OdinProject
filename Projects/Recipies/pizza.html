Pizza

Description

Pizza is one of my favourite dish, its so easy to make and here i will be elling you just how to make it!

Ingredients

  • Pizza Base
  • Cheese
  • Sauce
  • Veggies
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Firstly take your fresh pizza base and sauce it up evenly
  2. Now grat the cheese and spread little bil on the base
  3. Now add finely chopped veggies on top
  4. Then sprinkle the spices like oregano and chilli flakes
  5. Now put it in oven on maximum temperature and bake it for 10 mintues
  6. And theres your hot cheesy pizza, Ready to eat
